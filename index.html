1 How It Works

A small solar panel charges a 12V battery. A timer relay periodically switches on a diaphragm air pump for a few seconds, sending a burst of air through tubing aimed at the ledge above your balcony. The sudden puff startles pigeons and makes the ledge feel unsafe.
