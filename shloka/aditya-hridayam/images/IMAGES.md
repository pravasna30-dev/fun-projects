# Stanza Images — Sourcing & Credits

All images are real, public-domain or Creative-Commons-licensed works from
Wikimedia Commons (no AI-generated or synthesized images — no image
generation tool was available; see the shloka-companion skill for why).
Verified license/attribution status for each is below. Many chapters share
the same underlying image where the stanzas describe the same subject
(most of Verses 4–26 are lists of Surya's names/epithets/forms rather than
distinct narrative scenes, so full uniqueness per stanza isn't a meaningful
goal there — variety is given where the text actually changes: Rama/Ravana/
Agastya narrative verses, the Trimurti invocation, and the two closing
iconographic verses).
